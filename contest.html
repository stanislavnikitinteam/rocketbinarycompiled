Traiding Contest frequently asked questions

How the winning places are distributed?
The places are distributed based on number of trades in 1 week, during active contest time.
What are the prizes for this contest?
First 4 places are getting 20000$ in cash. Other places are getting valuable prizes and bonuses.
How do I sign up for the contest?
All Rocketbinary traders are automatically signed up for contest.
When will the competition be running?
The contest starts every Sunday at 21.00 GMT and ends Sunday at 20.30 GMT.
Is there any trading limitations to qualify in the contest?
All trades are counted, regardless trading result. The minimum trade size should be 1$. CFD trading volume is not counted in trading contest.
How do I sign up for the contest?
All Rocketbinary traders are automatically signed up for contest.
How will I know if I won a prize?
You will be notified by email about your place and the prize you won. Also you can always check your rank in the leader board.
How can I get a prize I won?
To get a prize, your available trading net balance should be equal to your minimum deposit or more. Bonus prize will be credited to a trading account, if total amount of previous bonuses is not exceeding 150% of the total deposits amount. Trader can also refuse to accept bonus prize to his trading account.
